Read up on your favorite player, or watch a stream.

Team SoloMid Browser is a desktop application that keeps TSM fans up to date with current news, social media updates, and streams from all of their favorite players. It also allows fans to simply watch any twitch user's stream, as well as participate in Twitch Chat. The application is free to download and will remain free forever, with constant updates based on fan and general user feedback.

    List of features:

  • Find your favorite TSM player and their biography/statistics.
  • Find their social media, and their social media updates.
  • Watch their streams and participate in their chat.
  • Find news and updates from the Organization themselves.
  • Watch any stream on Twitch, and participate in any chat.
WARNING!The Stream/Chat feature is a MASSIVE work in progress. Do not expect it to work well yet! Please send feedback as well as be patient while I optimize the code. Thank you!
